Privacy Policy

The text you enter into Word Counter is analyzed locally in your browser. We do not upload, store, or read that text.

Information we collect

The current website does not require registration or ask for personal information to use the word counting tool. If you contact us, we may receive the information you voluntarily include in your message.

How the tool processes text

Word, character, sentence, paragraph, reading-time, and keyword calculations run on your device using client-side JavaScript. Refreshing or closing the page clears entered text unless your browser independently restores form content.

Cookies and analytics

The current website does not set advertising cookies or use analytics services. If this changes, this policy will be updated before those services are introduced.

Third-party links

Our pages may link to third-party websites. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this policy.

Policy changes and contact

We may update this policy as the website evolves. The latest revision date will appear above. Questions can be submitted through our contact page.
