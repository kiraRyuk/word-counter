Frequently asked questions

Word Counter FAQ

What is an online word counter?

An online word counter calculates how many words are in your text. This advanced text counter also measures characters, sentences, paragraphs, reading time, and common keywords.

Is this word counter free and accurate?

Yes. This free online word counter is unlimited and requires no signup. It ignores extra whitespace, though Microsoft Word and Google Docs may treat special cases differently.

Can I use this word counter for essays and articles?

Yes. Students can check essay and academic paper requirements, while writers and bloggers can monitor article length, structure, and reading time.

How should I use keyword density for SEO?

Use keyword frequency to notice missing topics or repetition, not to chase a fixed percentage. Natural, helpful, and complete writing should always come first.
