About Word Counter

We make everyday text analysis fast, understandable, and accessible to everyone.

A practical writing companion

Word Counter was created for students, writers, editors, marketers, researchers, and professionals who need dependable text statistics without complicated software. Our free online word counter shows words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, reading time, and keyword density as you write.

Privacy-first by design

Your text is processed locally in your browser. The counter does not require an account and does not upload or save the text you enter. Read our privacy policy for more information.

Helpful metrics, not rigid rules

Good writing depends on purpose and audience. We present useful measurements that can guide editing, while encouraging every writer to prioritize clarity, accuracy, and a natural voice.

Have feedback or a feature idea? We would be glad to hear from you through our contact page.
